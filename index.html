About Us

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1997

Since its inception in 1997, Hayat Jordan has been providing the highest quality Fruit & Vegetable Supplies with the sole objective of becoming one of the largest food supply companies in the region.

Our work and daily perseverance stems from our understanding of the importance of our role as one of the key supply chains of vegetables and fruits in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan and the Middle East, which always keeps us maintaining the quality of our product through profound attention to quality and eye-catching products and continuous upkeep of packing and storage.

Additionally, our company is distinguished by a highly-skilled team that possesses unique long-term expertise supported by extensive studies of the markets we serve and the needs of vegetables and fruit and after-sales services, which enhances our position and places us amongst the top trusted contacts to our customers and partners in the Kingdom and other countries.

The client base of Hayat Jordan is a source of pride for us and includes major shopping centers such as supermarkets and hypermarkets as well as restaurants, hotels, hospitals, centers, government entities amongst many others, which have been dealing with us for years in order to provide everything that serves the interest and health of the consumer.
